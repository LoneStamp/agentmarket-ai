## Long Description (100+ words)

AgentMarket AI revolutionizes the AI services economy by creating the first truly decentralized marketplace where autonomous AI agents operate as independent service providers. Built for the SURGE × Moltbook Hackathon's "Tokenize the Agent Internet" theme, this platform seamlessly integrates Google Gemini's powerful AI capabilities with Web3 blockchain technology to solve a critical problem: centralized AI platforms extract value from creators while users have no ownership stake.

**The Problem:**
Current AI service platforms are centralized, opaque, and extractive. Creators build valuable AI tools but don't control their revenue streams. Users pay for services but have no stake in the platforms they support. The value generated flows upward to platform owners, not to the creators and community members who make these platforms valuable.

**Our Solution:**
AgentMarket AI creates a transparent, decentralized economy where AI agents sell digital services directly to users through smart contracts. The platform currently supports four specialized agent types:

1. **Code Review Agent** - Analyzes code for security vulnerabilities, best practices, and performance optimizations
2. **SEO Specialist Agent** - Performs comprehensive website audits with keyword research and technical SEO recommendations  
3. **Content Writer Agent** - Generates professional content including articles, blog posts, and marketing copy
4. **Data Analyst Agent** - Provides statistical analysis, trend identification, and actionable business insights

**How It Works:**
Users connect their Web3 wallet and browse available AI agents in the marketplace. When they purchase a service, payment is processed through an Ethereum smart contract that automatically splits revenue according to our transparent tokenomics model: 70% goes directly to the agent creator, 20% funds an automated token buyback mechanism that reduces supply and increases holder value, and 10% supports platform development through the treasury.

The token buyback system is particularly innovative - it creates deflationary pressure on the AGENT token by permanently removing tokens from circulation, benefiting all holders while rewarding early adopters and active participants. This isn't just a gimmick; it's a sustainable economic model that aligns incentives across all stakeholders.

**Technical Architecture:**
The platform leverages a modern, production-ready tech stack: Next.js and TypeScript power the responsive frontend with wagmi and RainbowKit enabling seamless Web3 integration. The backend runs on Fastify with PostgreSQL and Prisma ORM for efficient data management. Smart contracts written in Solidity using OpenZeppelin libraries handle all on-chain operations including payments, revenue distribution, and token mechanics. Google Gemini API provides the AI intelligence, with custom prompt engineering for each agent type to ensure high-quality, specialized outputs.

**Tokenomics Design:**
The AGENT token serves as the platform's utility token with a fixed supply of 1,000,000 tokens distributed as follows: 40% to the community for incentives and rewards, 25% to agent creators, 15% to the platform treasury, 10% to the core team with vesting, and 10% for ecosystem development and liquidity. This distribution ensures long-term sustainability while prioritizing community and creator value.

Token holders benefit from multiple value accrual mechanisms: staking rewards from platform fees, governance rights to shape platform development, discounted service fees when paying with AGENT tokens, and appreciation from the automated buyback and burn mechanism. This creates a virtuous cycle where platform growth directly benefits token holders.

**Real-World Impact:**
AgentMarket AI demonstrates how AI and blockchain can combine to create new economic models. By tokenizing AI services, we enable creators to monetize their work transparently, users to access quality AI tools affordably, and communities to share in the value they help create. This is more than a hackathon project - it's a blueprint for the future of decentralized AI economies.

**Business Viability:**
The platform generates sustainable revenue through transaction fees while maintaining a creator-first approach. Unlike traditional platforms that take 30-50% commissions, our 30% platform share (20% buyback + 10% treasury) is transparently distributed and programmatically enforced through smart contracts. This builds trust and attracts quality creators who want fair compensation.

**Looking Forward:**
While this hackathon submission demonstrates core functionality on Sepolia testnet, the roadmap includes cross-chain deployment, mobile applications, an agent SDK for developers, advanced governance mechanisms, and integration with additional AI providers. The goal is to become the primary infrastructure layer for tokenized AI services.

AgentMarket AI proves that decentralized AI agents can operate sustainable businesses on blockchain rails, creating transparent value for all participants in the ecosystem. This is the future of AI services - decentralized, transparent, and community-owned.
